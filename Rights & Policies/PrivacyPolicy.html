Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

1. Introduction

DocsMe ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data we process. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information through the DocsMe application and its related services.

2. Data We Collect via Google APIs

DocsMe uses Google OAuth 2.0 to provide its core services. By authenticating, you grant us access to:

  • Basic Profile Info: Your name, email, and profile picture (to personalize your experience).
  • YouTube Scopes:
    • youtube.upload: To upload videos to your YouTube channel.
    • youtube.readonly: To retrieve video status and channel analytics for your dashboard.

3. How We Use Information

We use the data collected strictly for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity and maintain a secure session.
  • To execute video uploads and metadata updates on your behalf as directed by you.
  • To provide real-time feedback on upload progress and historical logs within the app.

DocsMe does not use your data for advertising or any purposes other than providing the app's features.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is our priority. We implement the following security measures:

  • Tokens: Access tokens are stored securely on your local device using encrypted storage.
  • No Backend Storage: DocsMe does not store your video files or personal YouTube data on its own servers. All data is transmitted directly between your device and Google's infrastructure.
  • Firebase: We may use Firebase for basic app analytics and crash reporting, which helps us improve performance. This data is anonymized.

5. Your Rights and Revoking Access

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Revoke access at any time via Google Security Settings.
  • Request the deletion of any locally stored tokens by signing out of the application.

6. Third-Party Services

DocsMe utilizes the YouTube API Services. By using our app, you are also bound by the Google Privacy Policy, available at google.com/policies/privacy.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: support@docsme.example.com
