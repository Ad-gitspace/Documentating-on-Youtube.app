# DocsMe - Your YouTube Automation Partner

## Welcome to DocsMe
DocsMe is a powerful, streamlined application designed to simplify and automate your YouTube content workflow. Built with a focus on speed, security, and a premium "Glassmorphism" aesthetic, DocsMe allows creators to manage their daily uploads with ease.

### Key Features:
- **Seamless OAuth Integration:** Securely connect your Google/YouTube account.
- **Automated Uploads:** Handle video metadata and uploads directly from your mobile device.
- **Premium UI:** A modern, high-contrast interface designed for productivity.
- **Secure & Private:** Your data stays between you and Google.

### How it Works:
1. **Authenticate:** Sign in with your Google account.
2. **Select Content:** Pick videos from your gallery or use the built-in camera.
3. **Configure:** Set titles, descriptions, and tags or use templates.
4. **Upload:** Let DocsMe handle the heavy lifting while you track progress in real-time.
