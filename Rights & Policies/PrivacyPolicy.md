# Privacy Policy for DocsMe

**Effective Date: May 11, 2026**

DocsMe ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application to automate YouTube uploads.

## 1. Information We Collect
When you authenticate with DocsMe using your Google Account, we request access to the following information:
- **Basic Profile Information:** Your name and email address to identify your account.
- **YouTube Scopes:**
  - `youtube.upload`: To allow the app to upload videos to your YouTube channel on your behalf.
  - `youtube.readonly`: To view your channel information and upload status.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information collected solely to provide the core functionality of the app:
- To authenticate your session and maintain your login state.
- To upload video files you select to your authorized YouTube channel.
- To provide status updates and history of your uploads within the app.

**We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or YouTube data to third parties.**

## 3. Data Retention and Security
- **Local Storage:** OAuth tokens are stored securely on your device and are used only to communicate with Google APIs.
- **No Server-Side Storage:** DocsMe does not store your video files or personal data on its own servers. All interactions occur directly between your device and Google's servers.

## 4. Revoking Access
You can revoke DocsMe's access to your Google Account at any time through your Google Security Settings at [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).
